# How much does Convicta cost?

Convicta has four plans: **Free**, **News+ ($6/mo)**, **Pulse Pro ($12/mo)**, and **Pulse Edge ($20/mo)** — annual billing gives two months free. Right now Convicta is in **open community testing and everything is free**: every new user gets a two-month grant on the full-featured tier, because we are still collecting feedback and some features are not yet working exactly as intended. Nothing charges automatically — after your free period you choose a plan or stay on Free.

## What does each plan mean in practice?

- **Free** keeps you connected: the world-wires news preview, and 2 Pulse alerts covering the core alert types (price threshold, whale, trade, market resolution). Enough to see how Convicta works.
- **News+ ($6/mo · $60/yr)** is the full **Convicta News** product: all 1,600+ sources, your own sources and OPML import, personal keywords and filters, all 20 languages, and the daily digest. The Pulse side stays at the free level.
- **Pulse Pro ($12/mo · $120/yr)** is the full alerting product: unlimited alerts, every alert type, trader-watch, news→market triggers, and Pulse digests. The News side stays at teaser level.
- **Pulse Edge ($20/mo · $200/yr)** is everything: all of Pulse Pro, plus the experimental **Edge** AI fair-value estimate, plus advanced signals as they ship — **and it includes News+ in full**. There is no separate bundle to buy.

## What about the Chrome extension?

The [Chrome extension]({{ '/getting-started/chrome-extension/' | relative_url }}) is **free to install** and its anonymous surfaces (market widget, news, coverage dots) work without any account. Signed-in features follow the same plan as your account — for example, unlimited "Notify me" alerts follow Pro, and the full Edge display follows Pulse Edge. **During the current open testing period everything in the extension is free**, like the rest of the product.

## What is free during community testing?

Every new user currently starts with a **two-month free grant on the top tier** — the complete News + Pulse + Edge experience. Access is free right now because we are testing: we are collecting feedback and some features (particularly the AI-assisted ones — summaries, translations, the experimental Edge) are metered and may be limited while we tune them. When the testing period ends, your account simply drops to Free unless you subscribe — no automatic charges.

> **About Edge:** Edge is an AI-computed fair-value estimate in an early, experimental version; it improves as our models and data improve. It is **not financial advice** — it is one additional metric, and the decision is always yours. See [Features]({{ '/features/' | relative_url }}).

## How does payment and account linking work?

- Payments go through **CryptoPay** (@CryptoBot) inside Telegram.
- Web sign-in (Google / email / Telegram) ties the subscription to your account, so one plan applies in Telegram, on the web, and in the extension.
