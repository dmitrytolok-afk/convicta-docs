# How do I set up Convicta News?

Convicta News is a Telegram Mini App that turns 1,600+ news sources in 20 languages into one personal feed. Setup takes about two minutes: start the bot, pick a language, pick categories. This page walks through each step and tells you what is free versus paid.

## 1. Start the bot

Open **[@Convicta_news_bot](https://t.me/Convicta_news_bot)** in Telegram and tap **Start** (or send `/start`). The bot offers **"Set up your feed"**, which opens the Convicta News **Mini App** — the hub where everything is configured.

## 2. Get a feed instantly

You do not have to configure anything to see value. New users automatically receive the **World-wires preview** — a curated stream of world news — so your feed is never empty on day one.

## 3. Set your language

Pick your language in the Mini App. The interface follows your choice, and the **translate** setting controls whether news itself is delivered translated or in the original language — you can set it globally, per category, or per source (see the [Settings map]({{ '/settings/' | relative_url }})).

> Automatic translation is an AI-assisted feature and is metered during community testing. If it is temporarily limited, foreign-language stories appear in their original language — the feed itself keeps working.

## 4. Choose your categories

Select the **categories** you care about (you can start from presets). Convicta filters, de-duplicates, and tags incoming stories, so you get each story once, sorted into your topics — instead of a wall of near-identical reposts. Each card carries a "why you got this" context line.

## 5. Tune delivery (optional, recommended)

The defaults are sensible, but the feed becomes noticeably better once you set a few things:

- **Daily digest** — a once-a-day roundup instead of a running stream.
- **Quiet hours** — pause deliveries overnight; queued items arrive in the morning.
- **RSS group frequency** — per news group, choose instant delivery or a batched summary every 1–4 hours.

The full list of every setting, its default, and what "off" means is in the [Settings map]({{ '/settings/' | relative_url }}).

## What is free and what is paid?

- **Free:** the World-wires preview feed, category selection, deduplication, tagging, and "why you got this" context.
- **News+ (paid):** all sources, your own sources + OPML import, personal keywords and filters, the daily digest, and all languages. During the free-2-months testing period you can try everything. See [Pricing]({{ '/pricing/' | relative_url }}).

## How fast is the feed?

News processing runs on a regular **batch cadence** — Convicta is designed to be *timely and complete*, not millisecond real-time. Treat it as a feed that keeps you current, not a live ticker.
