# Getting started: Polymarket Pulse

Polymarket Pulse is **a community testing version** and is built for people who already trade on [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com). It turns breaking news into signals about the specific markets that news moves.

## Before you start

Pulse's personalization is built around your **Polymarket wallet** — the markets you hold and follow. To get the full experience you'll connect a Polymarket **0x wallet address**. If you don't have a Polymarket account yet, Pulse isn't the right starting point — try [Convicta News]({{ '/getting-started/convicta-news/' | relative_url }}) instead.

## 1. Start the bot

Open **[@Convicta_pulse_bot](https://t.me/Convicta_pulse_bot)** in Telegram and tap **Start** (or send `/start`). Use the **menu button** to open the Pulse Mini App.

## 2. Pick your language

Choose your language when prompted.

## 3. Connect your Polymarket wallet

Pulse asks you to connect your **Polymarket 0x wallet address**. This is a public address — it lets Pulse tailor signals to the markets you actually care about (the ones you hold or follow). Once connected, you get a personalized feed.

> **Prefer to look around first?** There is a read-only **"Browse Explore without a wallet"** path. It lets you browse markets without connecting anything, but it does not give you a personalized feed or personal alerts.

## 4. Your first signal

With your wallet connected, Pulse builds your personalized feed and can start alerting you. Depending on your plan, alerts include:

- **Price / threshold alerts** when a market crosses a level you set.
- **Market resolution status** — proposed, disputed, or resolved (with the dispute-window countdown).
- **Trader-activity signals** — activity from traders and wallets worth watching.
- **Edge** — an AI-assisted fair-value read on a market (see below).

**What Edge is:** an AI-computed fair-value estimate for a market — generated by our models, not a hand-set number. It gets more accurate over time as our models and data improve. Edge is **not financial advice**; it's an additional metric that may help you make a decision on prediction markets — the decision is yours.

> **Warming up during community testing:** the AI-assisted **Edge** read, and any news-derived summaries, are metered features that may be limited or paused while we test. The deterministic signals (price, resolution status, trader activity) are the core of what fires. See [Features]({{ '/features/' | relative_url }}).

## Free vs. paid during the testing phase

The Pulse free tier is intentionally light: **2 alerts**, no trader-watch, and no digests. Higher tiers unlock more alerts, trader-watch, and digests. While we're in community testing everyone starts with a **two-month free grant** so you can use the full experience — access is free right now because we're still collecting feedback and some features aren't yet fully polished. See [Pricing]({{ '/pricing/' | relative_url }}).

## A note on timing

Price and market-status alerts fire off live market data. **News-to-market matching runs on a batch cadence** — treat news-derived signals as timely, not millisecond real-time. We don't advertise real-time news alerts.
