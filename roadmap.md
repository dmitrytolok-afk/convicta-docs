# What is Convicta working on?

This page states our current development priorities at a high level. We keep it short deliberately: no dates, no feature specs — just the direction, so you know what to expect and what not to expect yet.

## Faster, cleaner notifications in your language

We are working on the speed and quality of notification delivery: getting each clean, de-duplicated, correctly matched item to you sooner, summarized and translated into your language. This covers the whole pipeline — ingestion, matching, summarization, translation, and delivery.

## Trade execution on top of signals — in development, not available

We are building automated trading execution on top of Convicta's signals — turning news→market alerts into an execution layer. This is **in development and not available**. Today Convicta informs decisions; it does not place trades, and nothing in the current product touches your funds.

## A better Edge

The **Edge** fair-value estimate is currently an early, **experimental** version. Improving its quality is ongoing work: better models, better data, better calibration. Edge remains an additional metric, not financial advice — see [Features]({{ '/features/' | relative_url }}).

## A wider universe, better matching

We continuously expand the universe of news sources and covered markets, and improve the quality of news→market matching — more entities, more relationship rules, better coverage — while keeping every match traceable to an explicit rule.
